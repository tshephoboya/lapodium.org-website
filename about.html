About

Founded in 2019 by a group of Landscape Professionals, Landscape Podium is platform for mentoring of students, growth of the landscape profession and grooming of candidates into professionals within the Landscape Profession. Every opportunity comes when there is a gap in the industry, in the case of Landscape Podium, it was established to introduce a mentoring eco-system where each member is given a platform for a dialogue in the Landscape industry.

South African history has left an undesired imbalance in the built environment as a result, opportunities are missed and those who wish to contribute in making a difference have not been given sufficient opportunities to speak about challenges they are facing in the built environment.

Landscape Podium is an entity that aims to create a dialogue in order to open opportunities for even those are still looking to become professionals or understand what the Landscape Industry is. And thus, in its literal sense of the word Landscape Podium, “it is your platform”.
